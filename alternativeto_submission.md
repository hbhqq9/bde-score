## Description

BDE Score™ is a free, open-source stock analysis platform that serves as an accessible alternative to expensive institutional tools like Bloomberg Terminal, FactSet, and Refinitiv Eikon. It covers 73 stocks across three major markets — US (NYSE/NASDAQ), Hong Kong (HKEX), and China A-shares (SSE/SZSE) — with a transparent, multi-factor AI scoring system.

Unlike Bloomberg's $20,000+/year subscription or FactSet's enterprise pricing, BDE Score delivers institutional-quality analysis for free or at a fraction of the cost. The scoring methodology is fully open and auditable — no black boxes.

### What makes it different from Bloomberg/FactSet:
- **Free and open source** (MIT License) vs. $10,000–$25,000/year proprietary licenses
- **Transparent scoring** — every factor and weight is published and auditable
- **No account required** — start analyzing stocks immediately
- **USDC payments** — borderless, crypto-native subscription for the Premium tier
- **EU AI Act compliant** — transparent AI disclosure, unlike most commercial tools

### What makes it different from Yahoo Finance / Google Finance:
- **Multi-factor BDE scoring** vs. basic price data and news aggregation
- **Multi-market coverage** (US + HK + CN) in a single unified platform
- **API-first design** with programmatic access for developers
- **Embeddable widgets** for websites and dashboards
- **Dynamic share cards** for social media

### Who uses it:
- Retail investors who want transparent, institutional-grade analysis
- Financial content creators embedding stock scores into their sites
- Developers building financial applications via the API
- Investors with exposure to US, Hong Kong, and China markets simultaneously
