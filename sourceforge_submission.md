## Short Description (200 words max)

BDE Score™ is an open-source, AI-powered stock analysis platform covering 73 stocks across three major markets: US (NYSE/NASDAQ), Hong Kong (HKEX), and China A-shares (SSE/SZSE). Unlike black-box tools, BDE Score provides a transparent, multi-factor scoring methodology that evaluates stocks across fundamental, technical, and sentiment dimensions — delivering a single, actionable BDE Score for each ticker.

Built with full regulatory compliance (EU AI Act Article 50), the platform offers free API access (3 queries/day) and a Premium tier ($29/month via USDC) for unlimited analysis, embeddable widgets, and dynamic share cards. Zero account required to start — users can immediately access analysis and begin scoring stocks.

Key differentiators:
- Multi-market coverage spanning US, Hong Kong, and China mainland exchanges
- Transparent, auditable scoring — no proprietary black boxes
- Open source under MIT license
- USDC payment support for global accessibility
- Embeddable widgets and dynamic share cards for content creators

## Features

- **Multi-market coverage**: NYSE, NASDAQ, HKEX, SSE, SZSE — 73 pre-analyzed stocks
- **Transparent multi-factor BDE scoring**: Combines fundamental, technical, and sentiment factors with clear methodology
- **Real-time market data integration**: Live pricing and financial data feeds
- **Free API access**: 3 queries per day, no account required
- **Premium API**: Unlimited queries at $29/month, payable via USDC
- **Embeddable widgets**: Drop-in score widgets for personal blogs, financial sites, and dashboards
- **Dynamic share cards**: Auto-generated visual cards for social sharing and presentations
- **EU AI Act Art.50 compliant**: Transparent AI disclosure, model documentation, and risk awareness
- **Zero friction onboarding**: No sign-up needed to access core analysis
- **Open source**: Full source code available under MIT License
