Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about BDE Score™

What is BDE Score™?

BDE Score™ is an AI-powered quantitative stock analysis system that provides transparent multi-factor scoring for 73 stocks across US, HK, and A-share markets. It evaluates each stock on 5 factors: Momentum, Mean Reversion, Volume, Volatility, and Trend, producing a composite score from 0-100.

How does BDE Score™ differ from other stock analysis tools?

Unlike black-box AI models, BDE Score™ is fully transparent — every score breaks down into 5 independently weighted factors. It covers 3 markets simultaneously (US, HK, A-shares), is EU AI Act Art.50 compliant by design, and is open source under MIT license.

Is BDE Score™ compliant with EU AI Act?

Yes. BDE Score™ is compliance-first by design. It meets EU AI Act Article 50 requirements effective August 2, 2026, with full audit trails, explainable methodology, and machine-readable compliance metadata. It does not make investment recommendations — it provides transparent quantitative signals.

What markets and stocks does BDE Score™ cover?

BDE Score™ covers 73 stocks: US markets (25 stocks including AAPL, NVDA, TSLA, MSFT, GOOG), Hong Kong markets (26 stocks including Tencent, Alibaba, BYD, Ping An), and China A-shares (23 stocks including Moutai, CATL, BYD, CMB).

How much does BDE Score™ cost?

BDE Score™ offers three tiers: Free (dashboard + 3 API queries/day), Premium ($29/month for unlimited API + 365-day history), and Institutional ($199/month for custom universe + compliance reports + SLA). USDC payments on Base chain are accepted.

Can I embed BDE Score™ on my website?

Yes. BDE Score™ provides embeddable widgets via iframe and a share card API that generates SVG score cards. Anyone can embed live stock scores on their website using a simple HTML snippet.

Does BDE Score™ provide financial advice?

No. BDE Score™ is a technical analysis tool that provides quantitative signals based on historical data patterns. It does not provide investment advice. All investment decisions should be made independently with professional financial advisors.

What is the BDE Score™ API?

The BDE Score™ API provides programmatic access to stock scores, market snapshots, and shareable score cards. Endpoints include /api/snapshot/{market} for full market data, /api/stock/{symbol} for individual stock analysis, and /share/{symbols} for SVG score card URLs.

What are the 5 scoring factors in BDE Score™?

BDE Score™ uses 5 factors: Momentum (price momentum and rate of change), Mean Reversion (oversold/overbought signals), Volume (trading volume patterns and money flow), Volatility (risk-adjusted returns), and Trend (moving average alignment). Each is independently scored and combined into a composite score.

Is BDE Score™ open source?

Yes. BDE Score™ is open source under the MIT license. The full codebase is available at github.com/hbhqq9/bde-score for audit, contribution, and self-hosting.
