## Full Description

BDE Score™ is an open-source, AI-powered stock analysis platform covering 73 stocks across three major markets: US (NYSE/NASDAQ), Hong Kong (HKEX), and China A-shares (SSE/SZSE).

**Why we built it:**
Bloomberg Terminal costs $20,000+/year. Most retail investors get their analysis from fragmented, black-box sources. We believe transparent, multi-market AI analysis should be accessible to everyone — not just institutions.

**How it works:**
BDE Score combines fundamental analysis, technical indicators, and sentiment data into a single, auditable score per stock. Every factor, weight, and calculation is published and open for review — no proprietary black boxes.

**Key features:**
- 🌍 Multi-market: NYSE, NASDAQ, HKEX, SSE, SZSE in one platform
- 📊 Transparent BDE scoring: auditable methodology, no black boxes
- 🔓 Zero friction: no account needed to start analyzing
- 🔌 API access: 3 free queries/day, unlimited for Premium ($29/mo via USDC)
- 🧩 Embeddable widgets: drop-in scores for your site or dashboard
- 🎴 Dynamic share cards: auto-generated visuals for social sharing
- 🇪🇺 EU AI Act Art.50 compliant: full AI transparency
- 💻 Open source: MIT License, auditable by anyone

**Try it now:** https://hbhqq9.github.io/bde-score/ — no sign-up, no credit card.

## First Comment (Founder's Story)

Hey Product Hunt! 👋

I built BDE Score™ because I was frustrated. As someone who follows markets across the US, Hong Kong, and China, I kept running into the same problem: every platform covers one market well, or covers all markets poorly. And the tools that do it well (Bloomberg, FactSet) cost more than most people's rent.

So I asked myself: what if stock analysis was open, transparent, and accessible?

**BDE Score™ is my answer.**

It's an AI-powered scoring system that evaluates stocks across fundamental, technical, and sentiment dimensions — across three markets — and gives you a single, transparent score. No black box. No proprietary magic. You can see exactly how every score is calculated, because the entire codebase is open source under MIT.

A few things I'm particularly proud of:

1. **No account required.** I hate it when tools gate their core experience behind a signup wall. With BDE Score, you go to the website and start analyzing. That's it.

2. **USDC payments.** Traditional payment infrastructure is a nightmare for global accessibility. By supporting USDC, anyone with a wallet can subscribe to Premium, regardless of their country or banking situation.

3. **EU AI Act compliance.** As AI regulation tightens globally, I wanted to build something that's compliant from day one. Article 50 requires transparency about AI systems — and that's exactly how BDE Score has always worked.

4. **Multi-market is hard.** US, HK, and A-share markets have different trading hours, different data formats, different regulatory environments. Getting them all working in one unified scoring system was a significant engineering challenge.

This is v1. I'm looking for feedback from the PH community — especially from people who follow multiple markets and want transparent, institutional-quality analysis without the institutional price tag.

Give it a try: https://hbhqq9.github.io/bde-score/

Would love to hear what you think! 🙏
