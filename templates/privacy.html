Privacy Policy

Effective: July 12, 2026

1. Information We Collect

BDE Score collects minimal data necessary to provide our API reliability assessment service:

  • Endpoint URLs: The URLs you submit for assessment
  • Technical Telemetry: Response times, HTTP status codes, header presence (automatically collected during assessment)
  • IP Addresses: Collected for rate limiting and abuse prevention only. IP addresses are retained for a maximum of 30 days and are not used for tracking or profiling.
  • API Keys: For authenticated users, API keys are stored securely and used solely for authentication and usage tracking

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide reliability assessments and scoring
  • Prevent abuse and enforce rate limits
  • Improve our assessment algorithms
  • Communicate with you about your account (if registered)

We do NOT:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Use your data for advertising or marketing
  • Profile individual users beyond service delivery

3. Data Retention

  • Assessment logs: Retained for 90 days for quality assurance
  • IP addresses: Retained for 30 days for abuse prevention
  • API keys: Retained until account deletion
  • Endpoint URLs: Aggregated statistics retained indefinitely; individual submissions deleted after 90 days

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • All data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3)
  • API keys hashed with bcrypt
  • Rate limiting to prevent abuse
  • Regular security audits
  • No sensitive data in logs

5. Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Cloudflare Tunnel: For secure access to our API
  • SQLite Database: Local data storage with WAL mode

These services have their own privacy policies. We do not share your data with them beyond what is necessary for service delivery.

7. International Transfers

BDE Score operates globally. By using our service, you consent to the transfer of your data to countries where our service providers operate, subject to appropriate safeguards.

8. Changes to This Policy

We will notify you of material changes via email (if registered) or through our service interface at least 30 days before changes take effect.

9. Contact Us

For privacy inquiries or to exercise your rights:

Email: privacy@bde-score.com
GitHub: https://github.com/hbhqq9/bde-score

10. EU AI Act Compliance

BDE Score is designed to help organizations comply with the EU AI Act. Our service itself is classified as a minimal-risk AI system and does not fall under the high-risk categories defined in Article 6 of the EU AI Act.

For transparency reports under Article 50, see our Dashboard.
