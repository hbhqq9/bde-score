## Description

### Overview
BDE Score™ is an AI-powered stock analysis platform that provides transparent, multi-factor scoring for 73 stocks across US (NYSE/NASDAQ), Hong Kong (HKEX), and China A-share (SSE/SZSE) markets. Designed as an open-source alternative to proprietary institutional tools, it combines fundamental analysis, technical indicators, and sentiment data into a single auditable BDE Score per ticker.

### Key Benefits for Financial Professionals
1. **Transparent Methodology**: Unlike black-box scoring models, BDE Score publishes its complete scoring methodology — every factor, weight, and calculation is auditable.
2. **Multi-Market Coverage**: Single platform spanning three major financial markets eliminates the need for separate tools per region.
3. **Zero-Friction Access**: No account creation, no credit card, no friction — analysts can immediately access stock scores and begin research.
4. **API-First Architecture**: Integrates into existing financial dashboards, research workflows, and portfolio management systems via RESTful API.
5. **Regulatory Compliance**: Built to EU AI Act Article 50 standards, ensuring transparent AI disclosure suitable for compliance-conscious organizations.

### Target Users
- Retail investors seeking institutional-grade analysis
- Financial bloggers and content creators (embeddable widgets)
- Quantitative analysts looking for transparent scoring baselines
- Portfolio managers needing multi-market coverage
- FinTech developers integrating stock analysis into applications

### Scoring Methodology
The BDE Score combines multiple dimensions:
- **Fundamental Analysis**: Financial ratios, earnings quality, balance sheet health
- **Technical Indicators**: Price momentum, volume patterns, trend analysis
- **Sentiment Analysis**: Market sentiment signals from public data sources

Each dimension is weighted and combined into a single BDE Score, with full methodology documentation available in the open-source repository.
