# Show HN: BDE Score™ – Open-source AI stock analysis for US/HK/A-share markets

I built BDE Score™, an AI-powered multi-market stock analysis tool that provides transparent, explainable scoring across 73 stocks in US, HK, and A-share (China) markets.

## Why?

Most stock analysis tools are either black-box AI or expensive terminals ($20k+/yr for Bloomberg). I wanted something transparent, multi-market, and open source.

## What it does

- **Multi-factor BDE scoring** (0-100): Momentum, Mean Reversion, Volume, Volatility, Trend
- **73 stocks** across 3 markets: US (25), HK (26), A-share (23)
- **Live API**: Get scores via REST API, embeddable widgets, or shareable SVG cards
- **EU AI Act Art.50 compliant**: Full audit trails, explainable methodology
- **Zero account required**: Dashboard and basic scores accessible immediately
