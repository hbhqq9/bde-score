Title: BDE Score: Open-Source Stock Analysis Tool Covering US, HK and Chinese Markets

Story:
A new open-source project called BDE Score (https://github.com/hbhqq9/bde-score) provides real-time multi-factor stock analysis across three major markets - US (25 stocks), Hong Kong (26), and China A-shares (23). The system generates a composite score from 0-100 based on momentum, volatility, volume, trend, and risk factors. Unlike Bloomberg or Refinitiv which cost $20K+/year, BDE Score is completely free with no signup required. It includes embeddable badges for websites, a GitHub Action for CI/CD integration, and is designed with EU AI Act transparency requirements in mind. The tech stack is Python/FastAPI with FutuOpenD for market data and SQLite for storage.
