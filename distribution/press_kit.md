## Product Overview

**BDE Score™** is an AI-powered, open-source stock analysis platform that provides transparent multi-factor scoring across 73 stocks in US, HK, and A-share (China) markets.

## Differentiators

### 1. Transparent Scoring (Not Black Box)
Every BDE Score breaks down into 5 explainable factors with visible weights. Users understand exactly why a stock scored the way it did.

### 2. Multi-Market Coverage
Single API covers NYSE, NASDAQ, HKEX, Shanghai SSE, and Shenzhen SZSE — no need for 3 separate tools.

### 3. AI Regulation Ready
Built from day one for EU AI Act compliance. Full audit trails, explainable methodology, machine-readable compliance metadata.
